Are you a skilled artisan without a certificate?

The National Industrial Training Authority (NITA) offers you a unique opportunity through the Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) program. RPL allows you to get formally certified for the skills you've gained through hands-on experience—whether in construction, plumbing, electrical work, hairdressing, or any other trade. You no longer need to start from scratch or attend years of training to prove what you already know.

With an RPL certificate from NITA, you can unlock better job opportunities, qualify for tenders, grow your business, or even pursue further studies. It's time your hard-earned skills got the recognition they deserve. Don't let lack of papers hold you back—get certified, get recognized, and take your career to the next level.
